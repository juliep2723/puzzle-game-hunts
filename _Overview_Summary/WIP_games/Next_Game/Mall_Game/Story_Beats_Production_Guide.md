## ðŸ“‹ PRODUCTION NOTES

### Character Voices
- **Maya**: Warm, urgent, worried, determined, grateful, encouraging
- **Lex**: Determined, nervous, relieved, grateful (in videos)

### Video Production
- **Portrait videos**: Maya's messages (close-up, handheld, intimate, emotional)
- **Landscape videos**: Rube Goldberg visuals, Lex's videos (polished, documentary style)
- **Rube Goldberg visuals**: Superimposed on mall locations, 80s retro aesthetic (colorful, playful but dangerous)
- **Foam visuals**: Pink, bubbly, thick, covering/damaging everything
- **Settings**: Lex's workspace, mall locations, control panel

### Key Production Elements
- **Rube Goldberg demonstration**: Must be clear and kid-friendly in Beat 1
- **Foam consequences**: Show food/clothes/tech ruined throughout
- **Backwards journey**: Emphasize tracing from end (food court) to start (pipes)
- **Progressive tension**: Each interim video shows more of chain, more foam, more urgency
- **Word collection**: Each word seems random until final phrase assembly
- **Tone**: Rube Goldberg activation = BAD NEWS, not celebration
