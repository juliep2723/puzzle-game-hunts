# Story Beats - Production Guide
## Complete Beat-by-Beat Production Instructions

**Game Flow**: Cambridgeside Galleria Mall (various locations) â†’ Food Court Celebration  
**Time**: ~60 minutes  
**Core Loop**: Maya asks â†’ Players solve â†’ Report back â†’ Maya reveals â†’ Next ask  
**Maya's Mechanic**: Collecting words from puzzles that form a phrase (revealed in Beat 6)

**Maya's Hinting Philosophy**:
