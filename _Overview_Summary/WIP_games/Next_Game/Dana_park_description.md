# Dana Park Landmarks

## The Emergency Call Box
**Object:** A tall, brown, 24-hour emergency pylon with a green light on top. It has a metal faceplate with a red button.
**Text & Key Details:**
* On the pylon (top, green section): "24"
* On the pylon (vertical, white letters): "EMERGENCY"
* On the pylon (lower down): Black graffiti (a heart and some lettering).
* On the metal panel (top): "GAI-TRONICS" (next to a 3D cube logo).
* On the metal panel (above button, red letters): "EMERGENCY"
* On the red button itself (white letters): "HELP"
* On the metal panel (black logo, bottom): "RED ALERT"
* On the metal panel (by small light): "CALL RECEIVED WHEN LIT"

**Crucial Detail:** There is a sticker of a white cat with black spots drinking from a blue bowl. The bowl has the word "MILK" written on it.

* "Established 1856"
* "By Edmund Trowbridge Dana"

## The Playground
**Context:** The main black metal fenced-in play area, which has different sections for different age groups.

* **Older Kids Section:**
    * **Object:** A dedicated area for older children.
    * **Key Details:** This area includes:
        * **Big-Kid Play Structure:** A green, multi-level children's play structure with slides and monkey bars.
        * **Big-Kid Swingset**
        * **Teeter-Totter (Seesaw):** A metal seesaw with two red seats and red handles.
        * **Spinning Toy**

* **Little Kids Section:**
    * **Object:** A separate, adjacent area for younger children.
    * **Key Details:** This area includes:
        * **Little Kids Play Structure:** A smaller play structure with a green double-slide. There is a large, yellow emblem of a sun mounted on the structure above the slide.
        * **Little Kids Swingset (Toddler/Bucket Swings)**
        * **Spinning Toy:** A small, green, bowl-shaped spinning ride for children.

## Ground-Level Art / Fixtures
* **5 Frog Medallions that squirt water from the ground / otherwise known as a splash pad:**
    * **Object:** A circular, reddish-brown metal medallion set into the speckled ground surface.
    * **Key Detail:** They each feature three stylized frogs arranged in a circle, their limbs interwoven with a Celtic-style knot pattern.
* **Patterned Ground Border:**
    * **Object:** A reddish-brown metal border strip set into the ground, running alongside the frog medallion splashpad.
    * **Key Detail:** The border has a repeating cut-out pattern of dragonflies and leaves.
